Student Apartments Around UW

Problem Description

Student living places, such as on-campus dorms or nearby apartments, are important to students college life. It relates to and affects many aspects of students' campus life. Some aspects are:

  • Convenience: Students who live on or near campus can easily access academic buildings, libraries, dining facilities, and other campus resources.
  • Safety & Security: On-campus residence halls can often provide more safer environment, they would have security measures such as ID card access systems, security guards, and surveillance cameras.
  • Engagement: Live near or on campus can make students more likely to participate in school activities, clubs, and events.
  • Reduced Costs: By living near campus, students can save on commuting costs, such as transportation costs and time costs that are spent traveling to and from campus.

Based on these value, we aim to creating an apartment finding app specifically designed for students at UW, making their housing search more convenient and efficient.

The information technology problem in this space is the lack of a centralized platform that comprehensively gathers and presents information about student apartments around campus. Students often struggle to find reliable, suitable and affordable apartments, making the search process time-consuming and frustrating.

There is a website "ApartmentsNearCampus", which was a platform for students to search for apartments located near campus. The website offers a searchable database of apartments, filters for specific preferences (e.g., price range, number of bedrooms), and lease signing. But the website did not provide a function for past residents or current residents to write reviews for a apartment. Which we think it is an important reference for a student approaching a new living place. And also this website is only made for off-canpus living near the University of Iowa, our website is going to focus on UW.

Proposed Application

  • Our target users of the application would be UW students, specifically those who are interested in housing near UW. Anyone who want to live in an apartment near UW could also use our websit as a reference.
  • The user can view different apartments next to UW, and see detailed information about these apartments, like basic information, distance of the apartments from school, floor plans the apartments offered, location of the apartments on a map, etc.
  • One interactive feature is that users can wrote reviews for different apartments, and rate these apartments based on their experience (1-5 stars). On the homepage of this website(Where users can check different apartments), users will be able to filter the resulting apartments based on distance, avg price, number of bedroom, etc. In addition, we plan to implement a search bar that will allow some simple level of searching. Also, some other features we may includes is: compare different apartments, sign in system and save any interested apartments.
  • By using this website and its features described above like search, filtering, rating, comments, students will be able to compare apartments, find the best suitable housing choice for themselves, and eventually solve the housing dilemma they often face when transitioning to a new city for their studies. It empowers them with the tools and information needed to make informed decisions, ensuring a smoother transition and a more comfortable living experience during their academic journey.
